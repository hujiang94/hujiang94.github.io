About me

I received my B.S. degree from Department of applied mathematics at Guiyang University(GYU) in 2013, Ph.D degree from Department of applied mathematics at Wuhan University(WHU) in 2018. I visited visited Prof. Bin Dong at BICMR of Peking University(PKU) from June, 2016 to Sep., 2017. Now, I am working at BICMR as a postdoc fellow.
